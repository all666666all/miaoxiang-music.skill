# 发行与冷启参考 (Distribution & Cold-Start)

How a new song gets traction on 汽水音乐 (a swipe-to-listen feed where the algorithm pushes songs to users). Read this before advising on 起量/冷启/被选用/搬砖. The five metrics below are the gates a new song must clear.

## The swipe-feed model

汽水 doesn't make users open playlists — the system pushes likely-liked songs into an up/down swipe feed. So whether a new song "gets heard" is decided by the first seconds and a handful of engagement signals. The five metrics compound: clear the early ones and the algorithm widens distribution.

---

## Metric 1 — 30s 跳出率 (first-ear)

**What:** of everyone the song is pushed to, how many swipe away before 30s. Those 30s ≈ 前奏 + 第一段主歌 + 副歌开头, and they decide whether users reach the climax and whether the algorithm pushes to the next batch.

**Fix — give a reason not to swipe in the first 30s:**
1. **前奏缩短，主题前置:** enter the vocal in **5–20s** (unless the intro is exceptionally catchy); show the catchiest hook / most distinctive timbre / most addictive groove inside 30s.
2. **情绪抓手，一秒入魂:** open with a repetitive, catchy lyric; or at 15–20s add an obvious "推进感" (drums enter / add a harmony layer / chord change).
3. **歌名+封面替你说话:** plain name with scene/emotion tag (《雪山自然冬日氛围》 → user knows it's for sleep before listening); cover = concrete scene image + big text ("考前一晚", "工地午休").

❌ Avoid: intro >15s of pure instrumental; flat emotion / 念白-heavy main vocal; ambiguous style (30s and you can't tell the genre); abstract-artistic name/cover.
✅ Want: vocal in <5s, chorus at 15–25s; distinctive timbre/melody up front; one 扎心/scene lyric to open; name+cover pointing at a specific 人群/场景.

---

## Metric 2 — 完播率

**What:** of those who listened, how many finished. High 完播 → algorithm trusts it → more pushes.

**Fix — manage the listener's patience:**
1. **结构与时长:** 2–3 min is friendly for a new song; if >3.5 min, trim a section.
2. **副歌别只出现一次:** repeat the hook before the end.
3. **果断砍掉无意义尾奏:** many swipe after the last sung line; a long instrumental 尾奏 drags 完播 down.
4. **中后段设"惊喜":** if many leave halfway the song is "太平了" — at Verse2/Bridge change the 唱法 (falsetto/气声), add a new harmony/instrument, or drop a catchy 间奏.

**A classic "抓人" front structure:** 0–15s distinctive 音效/器乐hook/抓耳清唱-念白 → 15–50s Verse1/2 (精炼歌词讲清场景/情绪) → 50–65s Pre-Chorus (情绪爬升, 鼓点加密/加和声) → 65–100s Chorus (在~1分钟准时亮出核心 hook) → 100–115s 间奏 (抓耳).

## Metric 3 — 互动率 (红心/评论/分享 ÷ 播放)

**What:** does the song move people enough to *act* — the 情绪试金石. In 冷启, even at low plays, a song whose 互动密度 clearly beats your others has 爆款潜质 — invest 宣推/二创 around it.

**Fix — interaction must be designed, not waited for:**
1. **歌词埋"评论钩子":** a line with discussion space ("你清空了我们的聊天记录吗？") makes people share their story.
2. **自己下场带评论区风向:** post the first comment from your own account — the backstory, or a question ("听到哪句词你最有感觉？").
3. **宣推视频文案:** "这首歌是写给____的，评论区填空."

(自带情怀/回忆 themes and 游戏/动漫二创 naturally pull comments.)

## Metric 4 — 收藏 / 使用率 (the BGM lever — most important for 搬砖)

**What:** 收藏/加入歌单 ("我想下次再听" → 耐听度高) and **使用率** ("别人拿你的歌当视频 BGM" → 场景适配, 爆款 BGM 潜力). For a 供曲/BGM creator, **使用率 is the money lever.**

**Fix:**
1. **强化场景标签:** state the use-scene in 歌名/简介/评论区 ("适合旅行 VLOG", "适合生日祝福", "跳手势舞绝了", "女神节快乐").
2. **主动做用法示范:** post several demo videos of the *same* song used different ways (大字报情绪文案 / 手势舞 / 风景混剪) — "我的歌可以这么用".
3. **发起合拍/使用挑战:** "用这首歌拍出你的家乡", "和我一起用这首歌卡点变装".

**Key:** 使用率 is search-driven — creators find your track by typing scene/emotion keywords. So discoverability is a *metadata* problem you control (name + tags), not an audio lottery. Two near-identical tracks with different tags get very different pickup.

---

## Metric 5 — 关注 / 转粉率 (歌红 → 人红)

**What:** new followers per 1000 plays. Did they follow *you*, or just like one song? If plays are high but 转粉 low, your 音乐人形象 is blurry.

**Fix — build a 人设 and give a reason to follow:**
1. **歌手名/简介 = 人设标签:** memorable name + one-line identity ("只唱 Lo-fi 助眠音乐的AI", "一个爱讲故事的虚拟大叔").
2. **系列感 over 东一榔头西一棒子:** unify 风格/音色/主题/封面/标题格式 so users recognize "这是那个谁的新作"; build IP-linked series.
3. **给出关注理由:** in 简介/视频结尾/评论区 — "如果你也喜欢赛博朋克风的歌，关注我，每周更新".

**结尾引导文案 templates:**
- 引导四连: "如果这首歌也说中了你的心事，别忘了点赞收藏，在评论区留下你的故事。想听完整版，可以去汽水音乐搜《歌名》，也别忘了关注我！"
- BGM 使用引导: "这首歌的 BGM 已经给你备好了，快点击视频下方'拍同款'，用它记录你的生活吧！"

## Naming + cover (serves both search and content review)

- **Name** by use-scene/emotion, plainly. Scene names (《下午四点的旧教室》) hit creator searches AND keep titles clean.
- **Cover** = concrete scene + big readable text; review rules forbid 明星照片/惊悚血腥/色情低俗 and ban 特殊符号/公司名/广告嫌疑词 in 音乐人名称.
- Avoid abstract/edgy titles — they both hurt discovery and raise content-review risk.

## Account operation (矩阵化)

矩阵化运营 is the norm: a **音乐账号** (汽水/音频平台) + a **视频账号** (抖音, drives 红心/使用率 via demo videos and 宣推). Unify a clear 人设 and do series content. 2025 爆款 patterns span 改编 (《跳楼机-黑人福音版》《大东北是我的家乡》), 原创 (《越界》, lyric-driven 共情), 翻唱 (R&B is AI's most mature style; 《泪桥》《你看月亮的脸》). Across all of them, a strong melody and an emotionally-resonant lyric are the precondition for going viral.

## For pure-instrumental / BGM 搬砖

- The money path is **使用率** (Metric 4), not algorithmic push (the feed favors mass-appeal vocal hooks; niche/ambient is weak there).
- 使用率 depends on **被搜中 → 被选用**, so optimize **name + scene tags** as hard as audio.
- Clear the **30s/60s** bar: get something distinctive in the first seconds; `≥60s` length; loopable; no dead intro/尾奏.
- Track per-song: name/tags, core attributes (BPM, 暗/暖, 有无旋律, 时长), 有效播放(>60s), 被选用次数. After a window, find the 2–3 that seeded, extract their shared template, generate the next batch toward it (generate → evaluate → converge).
